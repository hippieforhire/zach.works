Ultimate Wordle

How to Play

Guess the secret word in six tries. Each guess must be a valid word. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

  • Green: The letter is in the correct spot.
  • Yellow: The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • Gray: The letter is not in the word.

Good luck and have fun!
